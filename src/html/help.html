About Suika2 Kirara

Suika2 Kirara is a graphical implementation of the Suika2 visual novel development engine. The aim of this application is to provide a beginner friendly environment so that users are able to explore the world of visual novel creation in a structured, fail-safe way. To add to this simplicity, Kirara offers a simple-to-use export page so that creators can share their stories and games with their friends and family, or even distribute to web browsers and web-based platforms.
